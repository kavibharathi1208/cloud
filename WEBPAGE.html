KONGU ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Kongu Engineering College (KEC) is an autonomous institution located in Perundurai, Erode district, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1984, it is affiliated to Anna University and accredited with an 'A++' Grade by NAAC. KEC offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and management.

Courses Offered

  • B.E. Civil Engineering
  • B.E. Mechanical Engineering
  • B.E. Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.E. Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech. Information Technology
  • B.Tech. Food Technology
  • M.E. Structural Engineering
  • M.E. VLSI Design
  • MBA
  • MCA
